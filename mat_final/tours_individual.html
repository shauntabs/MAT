Guatape | Full Day (1-4 People)

The most popular destination for a day trip from Medellin. Climb the rock and visit the colorful town of Guatape. Want to enjoy a day out of the city? Guatape should be on top of your list!

Enjoy beautiful Guatapé
FULL DAY

Guatape is famous around the world as a must-see destination. The huge El Peñol rock towers over the magnificent man-made reservoir allowing the eye to see for miles and miles. However, make sure you bring you walking boots as it's one hell of a climb to the top. Zig zagging staircases make their way up to the top with tourists trudging all the way up.

After you can take a well-deserved rest aboard one of the many ferry boats that can take your around the lakes and see the beautiful islands and amazing houses that dot the shoreline. Be sure not to miss the little town that's filled with cute and colourful hoses that are traditional across the region.
