Our Fleet

All of our vehicles are modern, air-conditioned and comfortable. We only use vehicles that are suited to our service – making sure that you are safe, comfortable and on-time. In our growing fleet we have Tourist vehicles (4x4s) Vans, Minibuses, buses and 4-passenger cars. They are all kept to a meticulously high-standard of comfort, cleanliness and safety.

Partnerships

We work together with Medellín based Turiservice S.A.S. With their years of experience and excellent service we can ensure high quality transfers. All of our vehicles are registered with the ‘National Tourism Board’, ‘Ministry of Transport’ and National Transport Board of Colombia’.
