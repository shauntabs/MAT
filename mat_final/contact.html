About Us

We are dedicated to providing a safe, reliable and on time service for anyone travelling to or from Medellin’s airport. We have an extensive fleet of modern vehicles, friendly and professional drivers, fair pricing and a customer service that is always here to answer your questions.

We are a young company. We noticed that it was difficult to get a premium taxi service in Medellin, one that was always safe and accredited as well as friendly and helpful. We first started working with an established local transport company called Turiservice. Together we took time to choose the right drivers to make every customer at ease and then we added our bilingual drivers service to improve your experience further.

We have taken all the steps to make sure that every part of our business is up to the highest possible standard. This includes accrediting every vehicle with the Colombian Tourist and Transport Boards, hiring the only the most experienced and trustworthy drivers and offering our services at the best possible prices. If you have any more questions about us please do not hesitate to contact us today.
