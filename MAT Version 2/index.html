Our Vehicles

Medellin Airport Transfer’s modern vehicles are all of the highest standard and quality ensuring that your journey is comfortable and on-time. Find out more information and take a look through some of our vehicles.

We know what travellers want when arriving in a city after a long flight – a comfortable, hassle-free and safe service that drops you right outside your hotel or apartment. Medellin Airport Transfer offers you just that.

The Journey

JMC International Airport is small so you will have no problem finding your driver. The arrivals hall is directly outside the baggage reclaim area.

We monitor all of customer’s flights using flight tracking technology so there is no need to worry if you flight is delayed or cancelled. We will be there when you arrive. The journey is longer than many first think, but don’t worry , sit back and relax – it’s a beautiful journey!
